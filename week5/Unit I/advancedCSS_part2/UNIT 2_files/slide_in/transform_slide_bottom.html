Hamilton Island

Hamilton Island is one of Australia's favourite island holiday destinations, located in the heart of the Great Barrier Reef. As part of our Stay 5, Pay 4 holiday package you'll receive one nights' accommodation FREE when staying at the Reef View Hotel, Palm Bungalows or Beach Club during the applicable travel periods.

On Hamilton Island you can appreciate great food, fine wine, warm weather year round, stunning beaches and a range of activities, tours and cruises to the Great Barrier Reef, Whitehaven Beach and surrounding Whitsunday Islands. The only island in the Whitsundays with its own commercial jet airport and direct flights from Sydney, Brisbane, Melbourne and Cairns daily, getting to Australia's favourite island holiday destination has never been easier.
